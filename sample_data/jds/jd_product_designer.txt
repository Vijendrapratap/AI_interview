Job Title: Product Designer
Department: Design
Location: New York, NY (Hybrid)
Type: Full-time

About Run:
We are a creative studio building next-generation digital products. We value aesthetics, usability, and innovation. We are looking for a Product Designer who can turn complex problems into simple, beautiful solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Lead design projects from concept to launch.
- Create user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups.
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions.
- Maintain and evolve our internal design system.
- Collaborate with engineers and product managers to ensure high-quality implementation.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in product design (UI/UX).
- Strong portfolio showcasing your design process and visual skills.
- Proficiency in Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
- Experience with prototyping tools (ProtoPie, Principle).
- Basic understanding of frontend technologies (HTML/CSS) is a plus.
- Excellent communication and storytelling skills.

Nice to Have:
- Experience in motion design.
- Illustration skills.
