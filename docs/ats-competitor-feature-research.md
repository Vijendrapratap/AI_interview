## Most important and impactful features

### Core ATS
- Job requisitions
- Custom hiring stages
- Candidate profiles
- Kanban/list pipeline
- Bulk actions
- Stage movement and rejection reasons
- Notes, tags, ratings, and activity timeline

### AI resume screening
- Resume parsing
- Job-to-resume match score
- Requirement checklist
- Skill and experience gap analysis
- Explainable AI recommendation
- Human override and audit trail

### AI interviewing
- Async AI interview invite links
- Role-specific question generation
- Adaptive follow-ups
- Interview transcript
- Competency scoring
- Structured scorecards
- Recruiter review before decision

### Collaboration
- Hiring team members
- Interview kits
- Scorecards
- Feedback reminders
- Decision summaries
- Approval workflows

### Communication
- Candidate email templates
- Interview invites
- Rejection/shortlist emails
- Bulk communication drafts
- Status updates and reminders

### Analytics
- Time-to-hire
- Time-to-fill
- Stage bottlenecks
- Source quality
- Offer acceptance
- AI screen pass rate
- SLA risk

### Enterprise readiness
- RBAC
- Audit logs
- Candidate consent
- Data retention
- Bias-aware/anonymized screening
- SSO/SAML roadmap
- HRIS/job board/calendar/email integrations
